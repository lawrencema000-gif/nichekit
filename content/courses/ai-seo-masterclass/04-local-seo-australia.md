# Local SEO: Dominate Your Suburb

## Why Local SEO Matters for Australian Stores

46% of all Google searches have local intent. When someone searches "gift shop near me" or "candles Sydney", Google shows local results first — a map pack with 3 businesses.

Even if you're an online-only store, local SEO helps if you:
- Have a home office with a real address
- Do local deliveries or pickups
- Want to target specific Australian cities
- Attend local markets or events

## Google Business Profile (Free, Powerful)

This is the single most impactful thing you can do for local SEO.

### Setup (15 minutes)

1. Go to business.google.com
2. Add your business name, category, and address
3. Verify (Google sends a postcard with a code, or you can verify by phone)
4. Once verified, fill in EVERYTHING:

**Complete these fields:**
- Business name (exact match to your website)
- Category (choose the most specific one available)
- Address (even if home-based — you can hide the address and show service area)
- Phone number (Australian mobile is fine)
- Website URL
- Business hours
- Business description (250 words, include keywords naturally)
- Products/services with prices
- Photos (minimum 10 — products, workspace, you)

### Weekly Updates (5 min/week)

Post a Google Business update every week. Reuse your social media content:

```
Write a Google Business Profile post about [TOPIC].
Keep it under 150 words. Include a call to action 
(visit website / shop now / learn more).
Australian tone, mention [city] if relevant.
```

## Local Keywords Strategy

### City-Specific Pages

Create a page for each major city you target:

```
/sydney — "Buy [Product] in Sydney — Free Shipping"
/melbourne — "Buy [Product] in Melbourne — Same Day Dispatch"
/brisbane — "[Product] Brisbane — Australian Made"
```

Each page should have:
- H1 with city name + product
- 300-500 words of unique content about serving that city
- Local references (suburbs, landmarks, local events)
- Shipping info specific to that area

**AI prompt for city pages:**

```
Write a 400-word landing page for customers in [CITY], Australia 
searching for [PRODUCT].

Include:
- References to [CITY] suburbs and areas
- Local shipping times from our warehouse
- Why customers in [CITY] choose us
- 2-3 local references (popular areas, events, lifestyle)
- CTA to shop

Don't make it sound like you just swapped city names.
Each page should feel genuinely written for that city.
```

### Suburb-Level Keywords

This is the unconventional play. Nobody targets suburbs.

"Candles Bondi" gets almost no competition. "Fitness equipment Parramatta" — wide open. Create content (or at least mention) specific suburbs in your copy.

## Google Reviews

### Why They Matter
- Reviews directly affect your ranking in the map pack
- 93% of consumers read reviews before buying
- Businesses with 10+ reviews significantly outperform those without

### How to Get Them

After every purchase, send an email (Day 7 in your post-purchase sequence):

```
Subject: Quick favour? (30 seconds)

Hey [Name],

Hope you're loving your [product]!

If you have 30 seconds, a Google review would mean the world 
to our small Aussie business. It helps other people find us.

[DIRECT LINK TO YOUR GOOGLE REVIEW PAGE]

Even a few words + star rating makes a huge difference.

Cheers,
[Your Name]
```

**Get the direct review link:** Google your business → Click "Write a review" → Copy that URL.

Target: **5+ reviews in your first month, then 2-3 per month ongoing.**

## Local Directories

List your business on these Australian directories (free, 30 minutes total):

- **Yellow Pages** (yellowpages.com.au)
- **True Local** (truelocal.com.au)
- **Hotfrog** (hotfrog.com.au)
- **StartLocal** (startlocal.com.au)
- **Australian Business Directory** (australianbusinessdirectory.com.au)

**Critical:** Use the exact same business name, address, and phone number on every directory. Google cross-references these — inconsistencies hurt your ranking.

## Your Homework

1. Set up Google Business Profile (or complete it if started)
2. Add 10+ photos
3. Post your first Google Business update
4. Create city-specific pages for your top 2 target cities
5. Send review request emails to your existing customers
6. List on 3+ Australian directories
