# SEO in 10 Minutes (Everything You Need)

## What SEO Actually Is

SEO = getting your website to show up when people search Google. That's it. Everything else is detail.

When someone in Melbourne searches "handmade candles Australia", Google decides which websites to show. SEO is how you become one of them.

## The Only 3 Things That Matter

Forget the 200+ ranking factors people talk about. For a new store, only three things matter:

### 1. Content — Do You Have What They're Searching For?

If someone searches "best protein powder Australia" and you sell protein powder but your website only says "Buy protein powder — $49", Google has nothing to work with.

You need pages with real, useful content that matches what people search for.

### 2. Authority — Does Google Trust You?

New websites have zero authority. You build it by:
- Getting other websites to link to you (backlinks)
- Having real customers leave Google reviews
- Being consistently active (posting, updating, being alive)

This takes time. Months, not days. That's normal.

### 3. Technical — Can Google Actually Read Your Site?

If your site is slow, broken on mobile, or has errors — Google won't rank it. The good news: if you built your store with Next.js on Vercel (from our core course), your technical SEO is already excellent.

## Your SEO Timeline (Be Realistic)

| Timeframe | What Happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| Month 1 | Google discovers your site. Almost no organic traffic. |
| Month 2-3 | Some pages start appearing in search results (page 2-5). |
| Month 3-6 | If you're consistent, pages move to page 1 for low-competition keywords. |
| Month 6-12 | Real organic traffic starts flowing. Compounds over time. |

**SEO is a long game.** It's not a replacement for the tactics in Module 8 of the core course. It's the engine that builds over time so you eventually don't need those tactics.

## Australian SEO vs. Global SEO

Good news: Australia is less competitive than the US or UK for most niches. A keyword that's impossible to rank for in the US might be achievable in Australia within 3-6 months.

**Key differences:**
- Use `.com.au` domain if possible (signals Australian relevance)
- Target Australian cities and suburbs in your content
- Register on Google Business Profile with an Australian address
- Write in Australian English (organise, not organize; colour, not color)

## What AI Does for You

AI handles the tedious parts of SEO:
- **Keyword research** — finding what people search for (Module 2)
- **Content writing** — creating blog posts that rank (Module 3)
- **Meta tags** — writing titles and descriptions for every page
- **Schema markup** — technical stuff Google loves (you don't need to understand it)

You make the strategic decisions. AI does the grunt work.

## Your Homework

1. Search Google for your main product + "Australia" — who shows up on page 1? Those are your competitors.
2. Search your exact store name — does your site appear? If not, submit your sitemap to Google Search Console.
3. Install Google Search Console (free) — this is how you'll track your SEO progress.
