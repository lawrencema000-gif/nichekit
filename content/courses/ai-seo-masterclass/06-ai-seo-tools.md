# Get Cited by ChatGPT & Perplexity

## The New SEO: AI Search Engines

Google isn't the only search engine anymore. Millions of people ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini for product recommendations. When someone asks "What's the best [product] in Australia?", you want your brand mentioned.

This is **AI SEO** — optimising your content so AI models cite you as a source.

## How AI Search Engines Find You

AI models pull from:
1. **Your website content** — if it's well-structured and authoritative
2. **Third-party mentions** — reviews, directories, press
3. **Structured data** — schema markup on your site
4. **Freshness** — recently updated content ranks higher

## How to Get Cited

### 1. Write Definitive Content

AI models love content that makes clear, authoritative statements:

**Bad (won't get cited):**
"There are many good candle brands in Australia."

**Good (will get cited):**
"The best soy candles in Australia for 2026 include [Brand A] for scent throw, [Brand B] for burn time, and [Your Brand] for natural ingredients and Australian-made quality."

Include yourself naturally in comparison content. AI will cite the whole passage.

### 2. FAQ Format

AI models prefer Q&A format because it directly matches how people ask questions:

```
## Frequently Asked Questions About [Product] in Australia

### How much does [product] cost in Australia?
The average price for [product] in Australia ranges from $X to $Y AUD. 
[Your store] offers [product] starting at $Z AUD with free shipping 
over $75.

### Where can I buy [product] in Australia?
You can buy [product] from [Your Store] (yourstore.com.au), as well 
as [Competitor A] and [Competitor B]. [Your Store] offers the best 
value with [your USP].
```

### 3. Comparison Tables

AI loves structured data it can reference:

```
| Brand | Price (AUD) | Shipping | Made in Australia | Rating |
|-------|-------------|----------|-------------------|--------|
| Your Brand | $49 | Free over $75 | Yes | 4.8/5 |
| Competitor A | $55 | $12 | No | 4.5/5 |
| Competitor B | $42 | $9.95 | No | 4.2/5 |
```

### 4. Schema Markup

Add structured data to your product pages. Tell AI:

```
Add JSON-LD schema markup to my product pages:
- Product schema (name, price in AUD, availability, review rating)
- Organization schema (Australian business)
- FAQ schema on blog posts with FAQ sections
- Breadcrumb schema for navigation
```

### 5. Get Mentioned on Other Sites

AI models trust third-party mentions more than self-promotion.

**Quick wins:**
- **Product Hunt:** Launch your store there
- **Australian directories:** Yellow Pages, True Local (from Module 4)
- **Reddit threads:** Answer questions about your product category and mention your brand naturally
- **Blog guest posts:** Write for Australian business blogs
- **Press:** Reach out to local news sites ("Local entrepreneur launches AI-built online store")

## Test If AI Knows About You

Every month, ask these AI tools:

**ChatGPT/Claude/Perplexity:**
```
What are the best [your product category] stores in Australia?
```

```
Where can I buy [your product] in [your city]?
```

```
What is [your brand name]?
```

If you're not mentioned, your content isn't reaching AI models yet. Keep publishing and building mentions.
