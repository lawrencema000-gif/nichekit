# The Launch Checklist

## Don't Launch Until Everything Works

Launching with a broken checkout or missing page destroys trust instantly. Run through this checklist before telling anyone your store exists.

## Pre-Launch Checklist

### Store Basics
- [ ] Homepage loads fast (under 3 seconds)
- [ ] All product images display correctly
- [ ] Every product has a description, price, and buy button
- [ ] About page is written and linked
- [ ] Contact page or email is accessible
- [ ] Terms of Service page exists
- [ ] Privacy Policy page exists
- [ ] Shipping & Returns policy page exists
- [ ] Footer has links to all important pages
- [ ] Mobile — test every page on your phone

### Checkout & Payment
- [ ] Stripe is in LIVE mode (not test)
- [ ] Checkout works with a real card (buy a $1 test product from yourself)
- [ ] Order confirmation email sends after purchase
- [ ] Success page displays after payment
- [ ] Afterpay/Apple Pay/Google Pay work (if enabled)
- [ ] Refund process works (refund your test order in Stripe dashboard)

### Technical
- [ ] Site is deployed to Vercel (not just running locally)
- [ ] All environment variables are set on Vercel
- [ ] No broken links (click every link on your site)
- [ ] No placeholder text ("[Your Name]", "Lorem ipsum", etc.)
- [ ] Browser console has no errors (right-click > Inspect > Console)
- [ ] Google can find your site (search `site:your-store.vercel.app`)

### Legal (Australia)
- [ ] ABN displayed somewhere (footer or About page) — required if you have one
- [ ] Prices displayed in AUD
- [ ] Returns policy complies with Australian Consumer Law
- [ ] You're not making false claims about your product

## Soft Launch Strategy

Don't announce to the world on day one. Do a soft launch:

**Day 1-3: Friends & Family**
- Share the link with 5-10 people you trust
- Ask them to go through the entire purchase flow
- Collect honest feedback: "What confused you? What almost made you leave?"
- Fix any issues they find

**Day 4-7: Warm Network**
- Post on your personal social media
- Tell your email contacts
- Share in any groups or communities you're part of
- Goal: get your first 3-5 real sales

**Day 8+: Public Launch**
- Now you have a working store with real orders
- Social proof exists (even if it's small)
- You've fixed the obvious problems
- Time to scale (we cover this in Modules 8-10)

## The $1 Test

Before anything else, buy your own product:

1. Open your live store URL
2. Add a product to checkout
3. Pay with your real credit card
4. Verify you receive the confirmation email
5. Verify the product is delivered (digital download works, or shipping label created)
6. Check your Stripe dashboard — the payment should appear
7. Refund yourself from Stripe dashboard

If all 7 steps work, you're ready.

## Your Homework

1. Complete every item on the checklist above
2. Do the $1 test
3. Share your store link with 3 friends and collect feedback
4. Fix anything they flag
5. Take a screenshot of your first real Stripe payment — you earned it
