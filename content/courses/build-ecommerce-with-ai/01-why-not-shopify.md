# Why Not Shopify (And What to Use Instead)

## The Problem Nobody Talks About

Shopify costs **$79 AUD/month** on their Basic plan. That's $948/year before you sell a single thing. Add a theme ($350), apps for reviews ($15/mo), email ($20/mo), SEO ($30/mo), and you're looking at **$1,700+ in your first year** just to have a store that exists.

Then Shopify takes **2% of every transaction** on top of payment processing fees.

For a store doing $5,000/month in revenue, you're paying Shopify roughly **$200/month** in fees and subscriptions. That's $2,400/year — gone.

**There's a better way.**

## The Australian Advantage

Building your own store in Australia actually gives you advantages:

1. **ABN & GST control** — You handle GST your way, not Shopify's way. Register for GST when you hit $75K revenue, not before.
2. **Australian payment processors** — Stripe Australia has lower fees (1.75% + $0.30) than Shopify Payments (1.75% + $0.30 PLUS Shopify's monthly fee).
3. **Data sovereignty** — Your customer data stays where you want it. Important for Australian privacy law.
4. **No platform lock-in** — If Shopify raises prices (they have, twice in 3 years), you're stuck. Own your store and you're free.
