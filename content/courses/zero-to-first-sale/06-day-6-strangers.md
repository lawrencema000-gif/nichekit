# Day 6: Sell to Strangers

## Today: Beyond Your Network

Friends buy because they like you. Strangers buy because your product is good. Time to find out which one it is.

## Tactic 1: Facebook Groups (45 min)

1. Find 3 Facebook groups where your customer hangs out
2. Search for groups like: "[Your niche] Australia", "Australian [hobby/interest]", "Support Small Business Australia"
3. Don't post your store link immediately
4. Answer 2-3 questions in the group first
5. Then post:

```
Hey everyone! I just launched [product] for [target customer]. 
I'm offering 20% off for anyone in this group to celebrate 
the launch: [LAUNCH20]

[link]

Would love any feedback too — this is brand new!
```

## Tactic 2: Reddit (30 min)

Post in r/smallbusinessAU or a niche-relevant subreddit:

```
Title: "I built an online store using AI in a week — here's how"

Share your real journey. Be vulnerable. What was hard? What 
surprised you? Include your store link at the end, casually.
```

## Tactic 3: Instagram Outreach (30 min)

1. Search hashtags related to your product
2. Find 20 people who engage with similar content
3. Follow them, like 2-3 of their posts
4. DM the ones who seem most aligned:

```
Hey! Love your content about [topic]. I just launched [product] 
and I think you might dig it — would love your honest take: [link]
```

## Tactic 4: Create Urgency

Add a limited-time offer to your store:

```
Add a banner to the top of my homepage:
"Launch week special: 20% off everything. Ends Sunday."
Use coupon code LAUNCH20 (create in Stripe: Dashboard > Coupons)
```

Real deadlines make people act. "I'll buy it later" = they never come back.
