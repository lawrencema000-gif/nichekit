# Day 3: The Perfect Product Page

## Today: Make People Want to Buy

Your store exists. Now make it actually sell.

## The 5 Elements of a Product Page That Converts

### 1. Headline That States the Benefit
Not "Handmade Candle" — instead "Fill Your Home With Calm (Without the Chemicals)."

```
Rewrite my product headline. Current: "[YOUR PRODUCT NAME]"
Make it benefit-driven. What does the customer GET from this product?
Give me 5 options.
```

### 2. One Great Image
- Digital product: Canva mockup on a laptop screen
- Physical product: Clean photo, natural light, white or simple background
- Service: A graphic showing what's included

### 3. Description That Sells
```
Rewrite my product description using this structure:
1. Open with the customer's problem (1 sentence)
2. Introduce the product as the solution (1 sentence)  
3. Three bullet points of specific benefits (not features)
4. Social proof or credibility line
5. Clear call to action

Product: [YOUR PRODUCT]
Customer: [WHO BUYS THIS]
Price: $[PRICE] AUD
```

### 4. Trust Signals
Add below the buy button:
- "Free shipping over $75 AUD" (if applicable)
- "30-day money-back guarantee"
- "Australian owned & operated"
- Stripe/Afterpay logos

### 5. Clear Price + CTA
Price in large text. One buy button. No confusion.

## Today's Deliverable

Updated product page with all 5 elements. Ask someone: "Does this make you want to buy?" If they hesitate, ask why and fix it.
