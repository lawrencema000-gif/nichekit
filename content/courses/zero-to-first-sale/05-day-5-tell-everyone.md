# Day 5: Tell Everyone You Know

## Today: Your Warm Network

This is the day most people chicken out. Don't be most people.

## The 20-Message Rule

Message 20 people individually. Not a group post — individual messages.

### Script 1: Close Friends
```
Hey! I just launched a little online store. Would mean a lot 
if you could check it out: [link]. No pressure to buy — just 
want your honest opinion!
```

### Script 2: Acquaintances
```
Hey [name], I know this is random but I just launched an 
online store selling [product] and I'm looking for honest 
feedback from real people. Would you mind taking a look? [link]
```

### Script 3: Someone Who'd Actually Use It
```
Hey [name]! I remembered you mentioned [related topic]. I just 
built something you might actually find useful: [link]. 
Would love to know what you think.
```

## Social Media Announcement

Post on every platform you're active on:

```
I built an online store. It was terrifying and exciting 
and I'm putting it out there.

[PRODUCT] — for Australians who [BENEFIT].

If you have 30 seconds, I'd love for you to check it out: [link]

And if you know anyone who'd be into this, a share would 
mean the world.
```

Authentic beats polished. People support people who put themselves out there.

## Today's Deliverable

- 20 individual messages sent
- 1 social media post published
- Track responses in a spreadsheet

```

```
