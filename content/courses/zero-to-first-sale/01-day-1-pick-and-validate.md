# Day 1: Pick & Validate Your Product

## The Rules of This Course

1. One day per module. No skipping ahead.
2. Each day has one deliverable. Don't move on until it's done.
3. Speed beats perfection. "Good enough" ships. "Perfect" doesn't.
4. By Day 7, you will have made your first sale. That's the deal.

## Today's Mission: Pick Your Product

### The 30-Minute Decision

Set a timer for 30 minutes. By the time it goes off, you've picked your product.

**The fastest options for a first sale:**

1. **Digital template/guide** — write it today, sell it tomorrow, no inventory
2. **Print-on-demand** — design on Canva, Printful fulfils and ships
3. **Your expertise as a service** — sell a 1-hour consultation
4. **Curated product** — find something on AliExpress/Faire, sell at markup

**Ask yourself one question:** "What do I know that other people would pay $20-50 to learn or have?"

If you can't answer that, go with print-on-demand. Design a t-shirt. Seriously.

### Validate in 15 Minutes

Open these tabs:
1. **Etsy** — search your product. Results? Good. People buy this.
2. **Google Trends** (trends.google.com) — search your product. Trending up or stable? Good.
3. **Reddit** — search your product + "Australia". People asking about it? Good.

Three green lights = go. Don't overthink it. Your first product teaches you the game — it doesn't need to be your forever product.
