# One Piece of Content, 5 Platforms

## Work Once, Publish Everywhere

The biggest mistake: creating unique content for every platform. Instead, create one piece of great content and repurpose it across all platforms with minor adjustments.

## The Repurposing System

### Start With One "Pillar" Piece

Each week, create ONE substantial piece of content:
- A blog post (800-1500 words)
- A video (2-5 minutes)
- A detailed how-to guide
- A customer case study

This is your pillar. Everything else comes from it.

### Break It Into 5+ Pieces

**From a single blog post, you get:**

| Platform | Format | What |
|----------|--------|------|
| Instagram | Carousel | 5 key points from the post, one per slide |
| TikTok | Short video | 60-second version — read the key points or film yourself explaining |
| Facebook | Long text post | Copy the best 2 paragraphs + add a question at the end |
| LinkedIn | Text post | The business lesson or insight from the blog |
| Email | Newsletter snippet | Summary with link to full post |
| Twitter/X | Thread | 5-7 tweets, one per key point |
| Pinterest | Pin graphic | Quote or stat from the post on a branded background |

### AI Repurposing Prompt

```
I wrote this blog post: [PASTE BLOG POST]

Repurpose it into:
1. An Instagram carousel script (5 slides, punchy text, under 30 words per slide)
2. A TikTok video script (60 seconds, hook + 3 points + CTA)
3. A Facebook post (200 words, conversational, ends with a question)
4. A LinkedIn post (150 words, professional insight angle)
5. An email newsletter snippet (100 words + link to full post)
6. A Twitter/X thread (5 tweets, each standalone but connected)

Australian tone for all. Each should feel native to the platform, 
not like a copy-paste.
```

## The Weekly Content Calendar

| Day | Action | Time |
|-----|--------|------|
| Monday | Write 1 pillar blog post (AI-assisted) | 30 min |
| Monday | Run repurposing prompt | 10 min |
| Monday | Edit all repurposed content | 15 min |
| Monday | Schedule everything in Buffer/Later | 10 min |
| Tue-Sun | Auto-posts go out | 0 min |
| Daily | 15-min engagement routine | 15 min |

**Total weekly time: ~2 hours.** Content goes out 7 days across 5 platforms.

## Advanced: Video Repurposing

If you film one 3-5 minute video:

1. **Full video** → YouTube
2. **60-second clip** (best moment) → TikTok + Instagram Reels
3. **15-second clip** (the hook) → Instagram Story
4. **Audio** → Podcast snippet (if relevant)
5. **Transcript** → Blog post (AI cleans up the transcript)
6. **Quote graphic** → Instagram post + Pinterest

One video = 6+ pieces of content.

### AI Prompt for Video Transcripts

```
Here's a transcript of my video: [PASTE TRANSCRIPT]

Clean it up into:
1. A 1000-word blog post (structured with H2 headings, 
   remove ums/ahs, make it flow as written content)
2. 3 pull quotes for social media graphics
3. A 60-second TikTok script from the best segment
```

## The Monthly Rhythm

| Week | Pillar Content | Repurposed Pieces |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| Week 1 | Blog: "How to [tip for your niche]" | 6 social posts + newsletter |
| Week 2 | Video: Product demo or tutorial | 6 social posts + blog from transcript |
| Week 3 | Blog: "[Product] vs [Alternative]" | 6 social posts + newsletter |
| Week 4 | Customer story / case study | 6 social posts + email |

**4 pillar pieces = 24+ social posts per month.** Combined with your 30-post batch from Module 1, you have way more content than you need.

## Your Homework

1. Write one pillar blog post (or film a video)
2. Run the repurposing prompt
3. Edit the 5+ outputs
4. Schedule them across your platforms
5. Repeat next week — make it a habit
