# Engagement Without Being Online

## You Don't Need to Live on Social Media

Posting is handled by scheduling tools. But engagement — replying to comments, liking posts, following people — is what actually grows your account. Here's how to do it in 15 minutes a day, not 3 hours.

## The 15-Minute Daily Routine

### Minutes 1-5: Reply to Everything
- Open notifications on your primary platform
- Reply to every comment on your posts (even just a "thanks!" or an emoji)
- Reply to every DM
- The algorithm rewards accounts that reply quickly

### Minutes 5-10: Engage With Others
- Go to 5 accounts in your niche (competitors, complementary businesses, influencers)
- Leave a genuine comment on their latest post
- Not "Great post!" — something that shows you actually read it

### Minutes 10-15: Discover New People
- Search a hashtag related to your product
- Like + comment on 5 posts from people who look like your ideal customer
- Follow 3-5 accounts that genuinely interest you

**That's it. Set a timer. 15 minutes, then close the app.**

## Batch Your Engagement

If daily feels like too much, batch it:

**Tuesday + Thursday + Saturday:** 20 minutes each session. That's 1 hour per week total, covering all your engagement needs.

## The Comment Strategy

Good comments grow your follower count because:
1. The account owner sees you → might follow back
2. Their followers see your comment → might check your profile
3. The algorithm sees you engaging → shows your content to more people

### Comment Templates (Adapt, Don't Copy-Paste)

**On a product post:** "Love the [specific detail]. How long did it take to develop?"
**On a tip post:** "This is solid. We started doing [related thing] and it made a huge difference."
**On a personal story:** "Appreciate you sharing this. The bit about [detail] really resonated."

### What NOT to Comment
- "Follow us back!" (desperate)
- "Check out our page!" (spammy)
- "🔥🔥🔥" (lazy)
- Anything copy-pasted (people notice)

## Instagram-Specific: Stories Engagement

Stories are the easiest engagement hack:
- Reply to other people's stories with a relevant reaction
- Use polls and questions in your own stories
- Share other people's posts to your stories (they'll notice and appreciate it)

## TikTok-Specific: Duets and Stitches

- **Duet** relevant content in your niche with your take
- **Stitch** a popular video and add your expertise
- These ride the original video's audience to reach new people

## Automation Tools (Use Carefully)

Some tools automate engagement (auto-likes, auto-comments). **Be very careful:**

- Instagram actively bans accounts that use aggressive automation
- TikTok is even stricter
- Facebook will restrict your account

**Safe automation:** Scheduling posts (Buffer, Later)
**Risky automation:** Auto-liking, auto-following, auto-commenting
**Never do:** Buying followers, using bots to comment

The 15-minute manual routine is safer and more effective than any bot.

## Track What Works

Every 2 weeks, check:
- Which posts got the most engagement?
- What time of day works best?
- Which hashtags drove the most profile visits?
- Which comments on other accounts led to new followers?

Double down on what works. Stop what doesn't. Simple.

## Your Homework

1. Set a daily 15-minute timer for social engagement
2. Engage with 5 accounts in your niche today
3. Reply to every pending comment and DM
4. Do this consistently for 2 weeks before judging results
