# Auto-Scheduling Tools That Work

## Schedule Once, Post All Month

You have 30 posts ready. Now schedule them so they go out automatically.

## The Tools

### Buffer (Recommended for Beginners)

**Free tier:** 3 channels, 10 posts per channel queued
**Paid:** $6/mo per channel for unlimited scheduling
**Best for:** Simple scheduling across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn

**Setup:**
1. Sign up at buffer.com
2. Connect your social accounts
3. Upload your 30 posts
4. Set posting times (Buffer suggests optimal times)
5. Done — posts go out on schedule

### Later (More Features)

**Free tier:** 5 posts per month per platform
**Paid:** $25/mo for unlimited
**Best for:** Instagram-focused, visual planning with calendar view

### Publer (Budget Pick)

**Free tier:** 3 accounts, 10 scheduled posts
**Paid:** $12/mo for more
**Best for:** Bulk scheduling, CSV import

## The Optimal Posting Schedule (AEST)

Based on Australian audience data:

| Platform | Best Days | Best Times (AEST) | Posts/Week |
|----------|-----------|-------------------|-----------|
| Instagram | Tue-Fri | 11am-1pm, 7-9pm | 4-5 |
| TikTok | Wed-Sun | 7-10pm | 3-4 |
| Facebook | Wed-Fri | 1-4pm | 3-4 |
| LinkedIn | Tue-Thu | 8-10am | 2-3 |

**Start with 1 post per day across your top 2 platforms.** That's 14 posts per week from your 30-post batch.

## Bulk Upload Process

Most scheduling tools accept CSV imports:

```
Convert my 30 social media posts into a CSV with columns:
Date, Time, Platform, Post Text, Hashtags, Image (filename)

Start from [START DATE], one post per day.
Alternate platforms: Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook...
Times: use the optimal posting times for Australian audiences (AEST).
```

Upload the CSV → all 30 posts scheduled in 2 minutes.

## Automation Without Tools

If you don't want to pay for a scheduling tool:

**Instagram:** Use Meta Business Suite (free) — schedule posts and stories
**Facebook:** Meta Business Suite handles this too
**TikTok:** TikTok Studio (free) — schedule posts up to 10 days ahead
**LinkedIn:** Native scheduling — click the clock icon when composing

It's more manual but completely free.

## Your Homework

1. Pick a scheduling tool (Buffer free tier is fine to start)
2. Connect your social accounts
3. Upload your 30 posts
4. Set the posting schedule
5. Verify the first post goes out correctly
