# AI Content Generation at Scale

## Create a Month of Content in 30 Minutes

The goal: never stare at a blank screen wondering what to post. AI generates everything. You edit and schedule.

## The Batch Method

Don't create posts one at a time. Create 30 at once.

### The Master Prompt

```
Create 30 social media posts for my Australian [PRODUCT TYPE] store.

Business: [NAME]
Products: [WHAT YOU SELL]
Target audience: Australians who [DESCRIPTION]
Tone: casual Australian, authentic, not corporate

Create this mix:
- 8x Product highlights (different angles, benefits, use cases)
- 5x Customer scenarios ("Picture this: you're [situation]...")  
- 5x Tips related to our niche (genuinely useful, not selling)
- 4x Behind-the-scenes / brand story
- 3x Promotional (sale, discount, new arrival)
- 3x Australian-themed (seasons, events, culture)
- 2x Questions / engagement starters

For each post:
- Full post text (ready to copy-paste)
- 5 relevant hashtags
- Recommended platform (Instagram/TikTok/Facebook/LinkedIn)
- Best time to post (AEST)

Don't start any post with "Introducing" or "We're excited to announce."
No emojis in every sentence. Use them sparingly.
```

### Edit Pass (15 min)

Read through all 30 posts:
1. Delete any that sound robotic
2. Add personal details AI can't know ("we tested this at the Bondi markets last weekend...")
3. Replace generic phrases with specific ones
4. Make sure no two consecutive posts sound the same

## Content Types That Work in Australia

### 1. The Straight-Talking Product Post
Don't oversell. Australians see through hype immediately.

**Bad:** "Revolutionary, game-changing, must-have product that will transform your life!"
**Good:** "We made this because the options out there were rubbish. Here's what's different."

### 2. The Relatable Moment
```
Post: "That feeling when your [product] arrives and you realise 
you probably should've ordered two.

Happens to the best of us. Link in bio."
```

### 3. The Useful Tip (No Selling)
Share genuine value. People follow accounts that teach them something.

### 4. The Australian Angle
Reference Australian culture, weather, cities, events. It builds immediate connection.

```
"Melbourne winter starter pack: blanket, candle, and something 
warm to drink. We've got the candle sorted — link in bio."
```

### 5. The Behind-the-Scenes
Show the messy reality. Australians trust realness over polish.

## Platform-Specific Adjustments

After generating your 30 posts:

| Platform | Adjustment |
|----------|-----------|
| **Instagram** | Add 20-30 hashtags in first comment. Use carousel for tips. |
| **TikTok** | Rewrite as a spoken script (60-90 seconds). Add a hook in first 3 seconds. |
| **Facebook** | Keep hashtags to 3-5. Longer text is fine. Ask questions to drive comments. |
| **LinkedIn** | Professional angle. Focus on business lessons and tips. No hashtags needed. |

## Your Homework

1. Run the master prompt — generate 30 posts
2. Do the 15-minute edit pass
3. Organise them in a spreadsheet: Date | Platform | Post Text | Hashtags | Status
4. You now have a month of content ready to schedule (next module)
