# List Building Without Annoying Popups

## Grow Your Email List Without Making People Hate You

Giant popup on page load? Exit-intent modal? Full-screen takeover? These work — but they also make people hate your brand. There are better ways.

## Method 1: Inline Value Exchange

Place a signup form in the middle of useful content — not as an interruption, but as a natural next step.

**On blog posts:**
After a helpful section, add:
```
Want the complete checklist? Drop your email and I'll send 
it straight to your inbox. [email field] [Send]
```

**On product pages:**
Below the product description:
```
Not ready to buy? Get our free guide: "How to Choose the 
Right [Product]" — straight to your inbox. [email field] [Send]
```

## Method 2: Footer Capture (Always There)

Every page on your site should have an email capture in the footer:

```
Stay in the loop. Australian-made tips and offers, once a month. 
No spam, unsubscribe anytime. [email] [Subscribe]
```

Simple. Unobtrusive. Converts 0.5-1% of visitors — which adds up.

## Method 3: Checkout Opt-In

During Stripe checkout, customers provide their email. After purchase, add them to your list automatically (with consent):

```
Add a checkbox to my checkout flow:
☑ Send me tips and offers from [Store Name] (you can unsubscribe anytime)
Default: checked

If checked, add their email to the subscribers table after successful payment.
```

This is your highest-quality list growth — these people just gave you money.

## Method 4: Social Media Funnel

Turn social followers into email subscribers:

**Instagram bio:** "Free [thing] → link in bio"
**TikTok bio:** "Download our free [guide] ↓ [link]"
**Every social post (occasionally):** "Want the full guide? Link in bio."

The link goes to a simple landing page:
```
[Headline: Get Your Free [Thing]]
[One paragraph about what it is]
[Email field] [Send me the free [thing]]
```

## Method 5: The Quiz or Calculator

Interactive tools convert at 20-40% (compared to 1-3% for static forms).

```
Build a simple quiz on my site:

"Which [Product Type] is Right for You?"

3-4 questions (multiple choice)
At the end: "Here's your result: [Product recommendation]"
"Want the full breakdown? Enter your email."

Store their email + quiz results in Supabase.
This gives you segmentation data AND their email.
```

## What NOT to Do

- **No popup on page load.** Wait at least 30 seconds or until they've scrolled 50%.
- **No fake urgency.** "Only 3 guides left!" when it's a digital file is dishonest.
- **No required signup to browse.** Let people see your products without an email gate.
- **No buying email lists.** Ever. It's illegal in Australia under the Spam Act 2003, and it destroys your sender reputation.

## Australian Spam Act Compliance

In Australia, you must:
- Only email people who **consented** (opted in, purchased from you, or gave you their card at a market)
- Include your **business name and ABN** in every email
- Include an **unsubscribe link** that works immediately
- Only send emails that are **relevant** to what they signed up for

Violating the Spam Act can result in fines up to **$2.2 million AUD**. Don't mess with this.
